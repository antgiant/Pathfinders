What does this report do?
Generates printable student schedule cards for Honor Day. Each landscape page contains 4 cards (quadrants), and each card shows a student's personalized schedule with spaces for teacher initials to confirm completion.
Required CSV columns
First Name, Last Name, plus a column containing Club Name in its header, and columns with time patterns like 8:00-10:00 am for honors and Lunch - ... for lunch items.
